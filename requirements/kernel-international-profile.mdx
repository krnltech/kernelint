# Organizational Profile of Kernel International Limited

## 1. ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

### 1.1 Background

Kernel International Limited (KIL) is a multi-disciplinary research, training and consulting service provider organization. It was established initially as single ownership entity in 2013 as SR Consulting International for overseas training and consultancy management, which has subsequently been incorporated as a private limited company with the Registrar of Joint Stock Company, Bangladesh under the companies' act (act XVIII) of 1994 and re-named as Kernel International Ltd. in April 2016.

Like the word Kernel International limited, our company is in the gist and core of any management consultancy works. KIL provide consultancy services for many different sectors such as Training Management, IT, Architecture and Design, Engineering, Construction, Agriculture, etc.

KIL always focuses to embark upon development projects with maximum efforts and always tries to achieve the maximum level of precision with all the projects. Resource is and always will be a very crucial fact of the foundation of the world around us. For the betterment of humanity and its proper development, KIL is operating with all its capacity to develop sustainability with all the projects - from management to design; from technology to consultancy. For the sake of a better future KIL always works to provide best solutions in Technology sections. It always attempts to provide the best visual output for any of the projects. Aesthetics hold a very important phase for the project output and it always tries best to ensure the projects maximized aesthetical value.

### 1.2 Mission and Objectives of the Organization

The mission of Kernel International Limited is to develop an institutional framework of excellence with a view to rendering research and consulting services to government, nongovernment and private sector organizations. Kernel International Limited (KIL) is committed to develop professionalism in consulting services through enhancing institutional capability and by providing experts to various development partner i.e. government agencies, NGOs, private sectors, research and financial institutions. KIL would evolve as a sustainable research institution and consultancy service providing organization in Bangladesh and abroad by providing quality services and ensuring institutional growth and sustainability.

**GO GREEN** is its Moto and it focuses on sustainable development in any case of consultancy services.

#### Major objectives of KIL include:

1. To emerge and act as supportive consulting service providing organization and meet up and live up to expectation of the donors, government and non-government agencies by providing technical support;
2. To act as a platform of the professionals to enable them to offer their technical services;
3. To render appropriate, timely and need based support to government and development partners;
4. To act as a consultancy organization focused on several sectors i.e. Management Consultancy, Training/Exposure visits, Education, Design & Architecture etc.;
5. To assist in planning and policy formulation by undertaking specific research for the government and development partners;
6. To provide a platform for the young newcomers in the job sector to learn and educate themselves in a practical and effective consultancy & research environment;
7. To assist the public & private sector organizations in decision-making process. Who are involved in implementing development agenda and to help them achieving the best output;
8. To provide information and necessary support services to public and private sector agencies for undertaking baseline, monitoring and pre & post evaluation studies; and
9. To organize workshops, seminars, conferences in order to disseminate research findings and share ideas.
